Return Policy

  • Returns can only be made within the first 5(five) days after you placed your order

  • If the returned item does not pass inspection, it will be delivered back to you

  • A returned item package must contain everything that was in the original package sent by MITRENDI, failure to which the return shall be subject to denial.

  • All items must be unworn and unwashed.

  • Items that smell like smoke, cologne, or deodorant cannot be accepted

  • It may take up to 7 business days for returns to be processed. We however do our best to get back to you as soon as possible

  • Once an item has been accepted, you will be contacted by one of our agents for further direction
